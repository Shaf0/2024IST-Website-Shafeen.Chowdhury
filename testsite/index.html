What's the Queen Gambit?

Consits of whites queen moving d4, which black responds with d6, with white moving c4. if black takes the pawn also being dxc4,the queens gambit has been accepted, The Queen's Gambit Accepted is the third most popular option on Black's second move, after 2...e6 (the Queen's Gambit Declined) and 2...c6 (the Slav Defense).

Queens gambit declined?

the moves that make the queens gambit declined is 1. d4 d5 2.c4 e6
